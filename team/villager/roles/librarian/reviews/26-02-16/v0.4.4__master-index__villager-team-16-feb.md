## 8. New Role: Translator

### Status: PENDING CREATION

The Translator is the first Villager-exclusive role, defined in [v0.4.3 Translator Role Definition](../../../../../humans/dinis_cruz/briefs/02/16/v0.4.3__role-definition__translator.md).

### What Needs to Be Created

| Artifact | Path | Status |
|----------|------|--------|
| ROLE.md | `team/villager/roles/translator/ROLE.md` | Pending |
| Issues file | `team/villager/roles/translator/.issues/workstream-translation.issues` | Pending |

### Key Properties

- **Team:** Villager only (Explorer does not need translation)
- **Mission:** Language translation (en to pt-BR, pt-PT) and audience translation (same content for different personas)
- **Two types of translation:** Language/cultural and audience
- **Supported languages:** English (en), Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR), European Portuguese (pt-PT), Klingon (Easter egg)
- **Pipeline:** Content created in English to LLM translation to human reviewer to corrections to published
- **Cross-cutting:** Touches every other role's output that reaches users
- **Human reviewer requirement:** Non-negotiable -- LLM translation is a draft, human native speaker approves
- **Three-phase evolution:** Internal translation (now) to scale and market entry (near-term) to customer-facing translation product (future)
- **Non-English market hypothesis:** Bigger market opportunity in non-English-speaking countries
