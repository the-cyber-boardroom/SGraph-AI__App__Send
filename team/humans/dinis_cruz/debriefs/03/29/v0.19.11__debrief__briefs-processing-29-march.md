
- 8 pages in v0.3.0 (upload, download, browse, gallery, v, view, welcome, + room/vault from v0.2.x base not yet migrated)
- 15 Web Components with descriptions
- Deploy workflow reference corrected (v0.2.0/v0.2.1 → v0.3.0/v0.3.1)
