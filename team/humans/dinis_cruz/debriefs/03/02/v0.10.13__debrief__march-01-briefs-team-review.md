## Key Findings (Cross-Role Convergence)

### Finding 1: Post-Payment Token Delivery Is the #1 Blocker

All four user-facing roles (Sherpa, Ambassador, Designer, Dev) converge on the same issue: **a user pays GBP 5 and then enters an indefinite waiting period where they cannot use the product**. The payment notification pipeline brief proposes multi-channel alerts (email, SMS, WhatsApp) to the human for manual token creation, but this won't scale to 50-100 Challenge participants.

**Action:** Automate token provisioning on payment (Phase 2 of payment pipeline) or commit to a 2-hour SLA with manual provisioning for Wave 1.

### Finding 2: Challenge Campaign Cannot Launch on Current Site

The Sherpa documents 6 friction points. The Ambassador scores 3/11 components ready. The homepage says "Get Started Free" when the Challenge message will say "buy credits." The page redesign must ship first.

**Action:** Ship the homepage redesign (already reviewed on 26-03-02 by 5 roles) and wire Stripe to the hero CTA before any WhatsApp messages go out.

### Finding 3: Early Access Endpoint Needs Security Hardening

The AppSec review found 4 actionable issues in the implemented Early Access endpoint:
- **EA-04 (High):** No rate limiting — publicly accessible endpoint triggers email on every request
- **EA-02 (Medium):** HTML injection risk — user-supplied name/email interpolated into HTML email body without escaping
- **EA-01 (Medium):** No input length limits on name/email fields
- **EA-07 (Medium):** No bot protection (CAPTCHA/honeypot)

**Action:** Add `html.escape()` to `build_email_body()`, add max length checks (200 chars name, 254 chars email), add rate limiting.

### Finding 4: Logo SVG Has a Slash Angle Discrepancy

The Designer verified the logo specification against the shipped SVGs. The spec says the slash should be at 75 degrees. The SVG geometry computes to approximately 70 degrees. Minor but should be resolved.

### Finding 5: Challenge Launch Timeline

The Ambassador estimates **10-14 days** to Challenge launch (target 12-16 March 2026), with the following dependency chain:
1. Content review (remove inaccurate claims from website)
2. Wire Stripe link to pricing page CTA
3. Ship homepage redesign with "Buy Credits" primary CTA
4. Payment notification pipeline (or manual 2-hour SLA)
5. Finalise WhatsApp messages (must match homepage CTA text)
6. Canary test (one friend, full flow)
7. Challenge launch
