## The Proposition

A service business built on top of SG/Send's existing capabilities. Partners (people already in the agentic world) deliver professional content packs to clients on specific topics: CVs, business proposals, startup analysis, business intelligence, sector research. The infrastructure is SG/Send. The delivery vehicle is a vault or zip. The economics are clean: 50/50 split during active service delivery.

## Why This Works Now

The platform already has everything needed:

- Vault-based communication and storage (encrypted, versioned, shareable)
- Infographic generation (automated, sub-10-cent per image)
- Rich page layouts via `_page.json` (mini-websites from vault content)
- Document generation via LLM workflows (Claude, ChatGPT, OpenRouter models)
- Folder/zip sharing via simple tokens
- The agentic team methodology for structuring analysis

Most potential clients have never experienced any of this. The power of taking a seed document and producing a full analysis pack with infographics, structured briefs, and a browsable mini-site is genuinely novel to them.

## The Three-Tier Delivery Model

| Tier | What the Client Gets | Revenue Model |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Final deliverables** | Polished PDFs, final documents, specific outputs. "Here is your business analysis." | One-time fee per pack |
| **2. Source pack** | Everything: source materials, prompts, scripts, intermediate outputs, all the working files. The full vault/zip. | Higher one-time fee |
| **3. Edit and transform access** | Ability to open the pack, make changes, run further LLM transformations, generate new infographics, iterate on the content. | Per-usage (per download, per LLM transformation, per edit). Recurring revenue via token consumption. |

Tier 1 is traditional consulting deliverables. Tier 2 is "show your working." Tier 3 is the recurring revenue play: every edit, every transformation, every infographic regeneration consumes tokens through the platform at the 25% markup.

## The Production Workflow

```
Client provides seed document / brief / topic
        |
        v
Partner creates vault with seed materials
        |
        v
Agentic workflow: analysis, document generation, infographics
(Claude / ChatGPT / OpenRouter models)
        |
        v
Pack assembled in vault:
  - Final documents (PDFs, reports)
  - Infographics per key topic
  - Source materials and prompts
  - _page.json for browsable presentation
        |
        v
Share via simple token -> client sees rich mini-site
        |
        v
Tier 1: client gets final PDFs
Tier 2: client gets full vault/zip download
Tier 3: client gets edit access, ongoing transformations
```

## Partnership Model

- **50/50 revenue split** during active service delivery
- When the service engagement ends, the split ends. Clean, no trailing obligations.
- Partner brings the domain expertise and client relationships
- SG/Send provides the infrastructure, tools, and agentic methodology
- Multiple partners can operate in different sectors simultaneously

## Target Sectors

The model is sector-agnostic. Initial candidates:

- Business analysis and due diligence packs
- CV and career portfolio packs
- Startup pitch and investor pack creation
- Compliance and regulatory mapping
- Training and onboarding material generation
- Customer-facing documentation and knowledge bases

Each sector needs a tailored template vault (see: agentic team setup pack creator brief) but the workflow is identical.

## Connection to Platform Growth

Every pack delivered:

- Demonstrates the platform to a new potential user
- Creates a vault that may be edited (Tier 3), driving token consumption
- Generates infographics and documents via OpenRouter, generating billing
- Produces a shareable mini-site that acts as marketing for the platform

The service business is also a user acquisition channel.
