# Designer Review — sgraph.ai Website Redesign: Layout Proposals

**Version:** v0.21.9 | **Date:** 21 April 2026 | **Role:** Designer
**Scope:** Layout wireframes and design proposals for every major page of the redesign
**Responds to:** `v0.21.9__dev-brief__main-website-redesign-aws-deadline.md`, `v0.21.9__brief__positioning-decision-hero-line.md`, `v0.21.9__arch-brief__three-primitives-agentic-development.md`
**Design system:** Aurora (dark navy `#1A1A2E`, teal `#4ECDC4`, DM Sans) — extend existing `_common/css/style.css`
**Design principle:** Lead with the AWS infrastructure story. Show architecture, not just features.
