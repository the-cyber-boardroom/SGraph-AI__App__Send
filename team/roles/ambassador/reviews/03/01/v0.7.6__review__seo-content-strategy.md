# SEO Content Strategy Review -- SGraph AI Website

**Version:** v0.7.6 | **Date:** 2026-03-01 | **Role:** Ambassador | **Type:** Review
**Scope:** All 20 HTML pages in `sgraph_ai__website/en-gb/`
**Site URL:** https://sgraph.ai

---

## Executive Summary

The SGraph AI website has 20 pages with generally good content but significant SEO weaknesses. The primary issues are: inconsistent title tag formatting, missing brand name from several titles, titles that are too generic, meta descriptions that miss keyword opportunities, and major content gaps for high-traffic keywords. This review provides page-by-page recommendations and a prioritised action plan.

**Key findings:**
- 7 of 20 pages are missing the brand name "SGraph AI" from the title
- 5 pages use overly generic titles that will not rank (e.g. "Architecture", "Contact")
- Multiple pages risk keyword cannibalisation on "zero-knowledge encrypted file sharing"
- No dedicated pages for critical search terms: "secure file transfer", "end-to-end encryption", "WeTransfer alternative", "Firefox Send alternative"
- No `<meta name="robots">` or Open Graph tags on any page (technical SEO gap)
