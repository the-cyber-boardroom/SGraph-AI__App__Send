2. Draft 3 outreach message variants: WhatsApp (casual), LinkedIn (professional), email (personal)
3. Draft the manual token delivery email template ("Welcome, here is your access token")
4. Produce a funnel tracking template (outreach → accept → purchase → first transfer → feedback → re-purchase)
5. Send first 10 outreach messages; record responses
6. Document what worked, what did not, and recommend iteration

**Dependencies:**
- Stripe Checkout configured with GBP 5 one-time product (Dev action, B2)
- Payment success/cancel pages exist (EXISTS)

2. "What is AES-256-GCM?" — how SG/Send encrypts files, why AES-256-GCM is the gold standard, what "GCM" means for integrity
3. "What is PKI?" — public-key infrastructure explained for professionals who are not cryptographers
4. "Symmetric vs asymmetric encryption" — when each is used in SG/Send and why
5. "How does SG/Send protect you?" — the end-to-end user security story: what happens to your file from the moment you drag it into the browser to the moment the recipient decrypts it
6. A content map: which educational pages link to which product pages, and vice versa

**Dependencies:**
- Static site deployment pipeline (EXISTS)
- Dev to create HTML pages from the Ambassador's copy (standard workflow)
