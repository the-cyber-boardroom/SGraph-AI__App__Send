# v0.6.20 Dependency Audit: fastapi-mcp Addition

**Date:** 2026-02-25 | **Role:** Librarian | **Status:** URGENT REVIEW REQUIRED

---

## Summary

Adding `fastapi-mcp` (0.4.0) to `pyproject.toml` triggered **17 new package installs** and **6 updates**. This is the largest dependency expansion in the project's history, driven by a single feature (~27 lines of application code in `MCP__Setup.py`).

## Before/After

| Metric | Before | After | Delta |
|--------|--------|-------|-------|
| Direct dependencies in pyproject.toml | 5 | 6 | +1 |
| Total installed packages (est.) | ~52 | ~69 | +17 |
| New supply chain entry points | 0 | 17 | +17 |
