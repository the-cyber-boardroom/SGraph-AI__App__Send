## Part 4: Key Decisions Already Made (Do Not Revisit Without Reason)

From the Conductor brief — these are finalized:

| Decision | Outcome |
|----------|---------|
| Core differentiator | "Cannot" (architectural) + browsable without downloading |
| Hero headline | "Share files. Browse them. Nobody can read them. Not even us." |
| Proton Drive positioning | Do NOT claim E2EE exclusively — they also have it. Our edge is browsing UX + no account |
| Privacy policy | Six sentences, visible on homepage, not just linked |
| Voice | "Cannot" not "will not" always |
| Pages to remove | /agents/ and /architecture/ — only AFTER /security/ is live and indexed |
| Live demo | `<sg-public-viewer>` component on hero — demo vault with intentionally public key |
| Nav structure | Features \| Security \| Pricing \| Tools + "Already have a token? →" |
