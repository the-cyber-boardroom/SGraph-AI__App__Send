### 5. LLM Efficiency Principles (v0.11.25)

**Status:** Principles codified (7 principles), companion article commissioned.

**7 Principles:**
1. One-shot requests (no accumulated history)
2. Minimum viable context
3. Use LLMs to not use LLMs (LLM-as-author, not LLM-as-runtime)
4. Briefs before execution
5. Not every agent runs every day (micro-teams)
6. Track externalities (Accountant role)
7. Wardley Map evolution (Genesis → Commodity)

**New role implied:** Accountant (cost tracking).
