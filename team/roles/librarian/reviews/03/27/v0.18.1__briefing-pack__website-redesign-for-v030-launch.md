## 5. The Six-Sentence Privacy Policy (Marketing Asset)

From the 03/12 brief — this is not just a legal page, it's a marketing weapon:

> 1. We encrypt your files in your browser before upload.
> 2. We cannot decrypt your files because we never have the key.
> 3. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on the product.
> 4. We store only encrypted ciphertext and a hashed IP address.
> 5. We delete your data after the retention period you chose.
> 6. That's everything. There is nothing else.

**How to use on the website:**
- Display the FULL policy text on the homepage (not just linked — visible)
- Comparison: "Industry standard: 4,000+ words. Ours: 6 sentences."
- Policy page shareable via QR code and fits in a social media post
- "Verify it yourself" prompt — encourage users to open DevTools → Cookies
- Zero cookies means NO cookie banner, NO consent popup — itself a differentiator

## 9. Design Principles

**From the master brief + Designer role definition (03/09):**

1. **Show the product, don't describe it.** Screenshots and interactive demos > paragraphs of text
2. **Security without austerity.** Trustworthy but not intimidating
3. **Progressive disclosure.** Homepage: 30 seconds. Features: details. Security: depth
4. **The recipient journey is paramount.** They're one click from sgraph.ai
5. **Form follows function.** Every element earns its place
6. **Less but better.** Don't overwhelm — curate
7. **Honest over polished.** Real screenshots, real features, no mockups
8. **Breathing room.** Generous whitespace, scannable layout

**Technical constraints:**
- Static HTML/CSS/JS on S3/CloudFront (same infra as send.sgraph.ai)
- Same brand (dark theme, DM Sans, teal accent) — sites must feel unified
- Mobile-responsive (many recipients open on phones)
- Multi-language ready (same i18n framework)
- Web Components with CSS custom properties for all visual values
