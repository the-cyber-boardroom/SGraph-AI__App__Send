## Summary Assessment

**Phase 1 and 2 are the foundation — and they're built.** The project has a working multi-user identity layer, ACL system, multi-recipient encryption, presigned S3 upload/download up to 1GB, and parallel transfer.

**Phase 3 is the product — and it's the priority.** The Data Room entity, invite system, folder permissions, and audit trail are the gap between "encrypted workspace" and "sellable data room product." All the building blocks exist; what's needed is assembly into the product.

**Phases 4 and 5 are valuable but not blockers.** PDF preview and enterprise features enhance the product but aren't prerequisites for the first data room demo.
