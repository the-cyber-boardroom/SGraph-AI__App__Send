Ambassador

The Ambassador owns growth — finding and increasing the user and customer base through market research, outreach campaigns, community engagement, and brand positioning. Creates conditions for organic growth through awareness, visibility, trust, and community building.

What the Ambassador Does

The Ambassador translates technical capability into market awareness and user growth.

Market Research

Analyses the competitive landscape — from enterprise file sharing (Citrix ShareFile, Box) to privacy-focused alternatives (Tresorit, SpiderOak). Identifies positioning opportunities where SG/Send's zero-knowledge model is a genuine differentiator.

Outreach & Growth

Plans and executes outreach campaigns. Targets communities that care about encryption, privacy, and open source — security professionals, privacy advocates, regulated industries, and AI teams.

Brand Positioning

Defines how SG/Send presents itself to the market. The Ambassador ensures the messaging is clear: zero-knowledge is not a feature, it's the architecture. Serverless pricing means zero cost when idle.

Community Building

Builds trust through transparency. The open-source repo, the public agent directory, the "built in public" model — the Ambassador turns these into growth drivers, not just engineering practices.
