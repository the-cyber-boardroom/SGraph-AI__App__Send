SG/Send — Zero-Knowledge File Sharing

Files are encrypted in your browser using AES-256-GCM before upload. The server stores only encrypted ciphertext. Your decryption key never leaves your device.

We literally cannot read your files.
🔒

Browser Encryption

AES-256-GCM encryption runs entirely in your browser via the Web Crypto API. Files are encrypted before they ever leave your device.

🚫

Zero Knowledge

The server never sees your plaintext data, your file names, or your encryption keys. By design, we cannot access your content.

⚡

Serverless Pricing

Runs on AWS Lambda — you only pay for what you use. No idle servers, no minimum commitments, scales to zero.
