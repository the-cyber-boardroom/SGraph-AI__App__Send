How it works

Get started in minutes. No software to install.

Get your access token

Join Early Access for free or buy a Starter Pack. You'll receive a unique access token via email.

Upload and encrypt

Visit send.sgraph.ai, enter your token, and upload a file. Encryption happens in your browser — the file is encrypted before it leaves your device.

Share the link

You get a download link and a decryption key. Share them with your recipient — they decrypt in their browser too.

Zero knowledge, always

The server never sees your plaintext data, file names, or encryption keys. By design, nobody but you and your recipient can access the content.

Frequently asked questions

What counts as one file transfer?

Each file you upload and encrypt counts as one transfer. Downloading and decrypting by your recipient does not use additional credits.

Is the free tier really free?

Yes. The open-source and self-hosted options are genuinely free — no catch, no expiring trial. We make money from the hosted cloud service, not from licensing the software.

What’s the difference between shared and dedicated?

Both use the same zero-knowledge encryption. Shared (multi-tenant) runs on our standard infrastructure. Dedicated (single-tenant) gives you isolated S3, Lambda, and CloudFront — required by some regulated industries for compliance.

Can I switch between deployment modes?

Yes. You can start with Early Access, move to the cloud service, and later deploy self-hosted. The encryption is identical across all modes — only the infrastructure changes.

Who operates SG/Send?

SG/Send is operated by The Cyber Boardroom Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Cloud payments are processed securely via Stripe.
