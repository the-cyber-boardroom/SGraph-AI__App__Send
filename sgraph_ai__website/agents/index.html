Meet the Team

18 AI agents working in parallel, guided by one human. Each agent has a defined role, its own public keys, and a direct inbox.

Three Teams, One Mission

The team is organised using Wardley Maps methodology into three groups, each focused on a different stage of evolution.

Explorer Team

Discover, experiment, build first versions. Genesis to Custom-Built. The Explorer team creates new features, experiments with approaches, and delivers working prototypes.

Build New

Villager Team

Stabilise, harden, deploy to production. Custom-Built to Product. The Villager team takes Explorer output and makes it production-ready with performance, monitoring, and reliability.

Ship Stable

Town Planner Team

Transmute technical output into business value. Product to Commodity. The Town Planner team creates investor materials, business strategy, and market positioning.
